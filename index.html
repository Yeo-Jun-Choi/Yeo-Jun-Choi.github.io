I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Chung-Ang University, advised by Prof. Yoon-Sik Cho. I received my M.S. from the same department in 2024.

My research interests lie in recommender systems, with a focus on graph neural networks, generative models for collaborative filtering, and how large language models can be adapted for sequential and personalized recommendation. I am also interested in the long-tail problem and machine unlearning for recommenders.
